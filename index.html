What's Included

All apartments feature modern amenities for a comfortable stay.

🛏️ Comfortable Bedrooms

All apartments include comfortable bedrooms with quality linens and sleeping arrangements for the specified number of guests.

🍳 Fully Equipped Kitchen

Each apartment features a complete kitchen with modern appliances, cookware, and all essentials for preparing meals during your stay.

🧹 Cleaning Service

Professional cleaning is included with every booking. The cleaning fee covers thorough preparation before your arrival and cleaning after your departure.

🌐 Free Wi-Fi & Utilities

Stay connected with complimentary high-speed internet access. All utilities including heating, electricity, and water are included in your stay.

🚗 Parking Available

Convenient on-site parking is available for guests. No need to worry about finding parking in the area.

📞 Local Support

Our hosts Christine and Roland Pfeiffer are available to assist with any questions or recommendations for your stay in the beautiful Allgäu region.

Pricing Information:

  • Prices shown are per night and include cleaning service
  • High season rates apply during peak periods (summer and holidays)
  • Low season rates available during off-peak times
  • Minimum stay: 3 nights for all apartments
  • Local tourist tax may apply and is paid directly to the municipality
